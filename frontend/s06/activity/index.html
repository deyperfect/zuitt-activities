Conan Edogawa

Who is Conan Edogawa?

Conan Edogawa is the main character of the Japanese mystery series Detective Conan. He was originally a famous high school detective known as Shinichi Kudo, whose exceptional deductive skills earned him widespread recognition. After being forced to ingest a mysterious drug created by a criminal organization, his body was transformed into that of a young child. To protect his identity and those around him, he adopts the alias Conan Edogawa and continues solving complex criminal cases in secret. Despite his childlike appearance, Conan relies on logic, keen observation, and advanced problem-solving skills to uncover the truth behind difficult crimes, all while searching for a way to return to his original form.
